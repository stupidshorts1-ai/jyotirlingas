The 12 Jyotirlingas of Mahadev

हर हर महादेव!

baba Kedarnath

The Lord of the Himalayas

महाद्रिपार्श्वे च तटे रमन्तं सम्पूज्यमानं सततं मुनीन्द्रैः ।
सुरासुरैर्यक्ष महोरगाढ्यैः केदारमीशं शिवमेकमीडे ॥

I offer my prayers to Lord Kedarnath, the benevolent one, who resides forever on the slopes of Kedara Peak near the great Himalayas, and who is worshipped by the foremost of sages, as well as by gods, demons, yakshas, ​​and great serpents.
