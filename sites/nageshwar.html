The 12 Jyotirlingas of Mahadev

हर हर महादेव!

Shri Nageshwar

The Slayer of Evil

याम्ये सदङ्गे नगरेऽतिरम्ये विभूषिताङ्गं विविधैश्च भोगैः ।
सद्भक्तिमुक्तिप्रदमीशमेकं श्रीनागनाथं शरणं प्रपद्ये ॥

I take refuge in Lord Shri Naganath, who resides in the extremely beautiful city of Sadanga in the southern direction, enjoying various pleasures and adorned with beautiful ornaments, and who alone bestows both true devotion and liberation.
