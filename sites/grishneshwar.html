The 12 Jyotirlingas of Mahadev

हर हर महादेव!

Shri Grishneshwar

The Lord of Compassion

इलापुरे रम्यविशालकेऽस्मिन् समुल्लसन्तं च जगद्वरेण्यम् ।
वन्दे महोदारतरस्वभावं घृष्णेश्वराख्यं शरणम् प्रपद्ये ॥

I take refuge in Lord Ghrishneshwar, the luminous form of Lord Shiva, who is seated in the picturesque temple of Elapura and is worshipped by the entire world, and whose nature is exceedingly benevolent.
