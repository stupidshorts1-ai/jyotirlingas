The 12 Jyotirlingas of Mahadev

हर हर महादेव!

Shri Trimbakeshwar

The Manifestation of the Trinity

सह्याद्रिशीर्षे विमले वसन्तं गोदावरितीरपवित्रदेशे ।
यद्धर्शनात्पातकमाशु नाशं प्रयाति तं त्र्यम्बकमीशमीडे ॥

I praise Lord Trimbakeshwar, who resides on the pure peak of the Sahyadri mountain range in the region of the Godavari river, and whose mere sight quickly destroys all sins.
