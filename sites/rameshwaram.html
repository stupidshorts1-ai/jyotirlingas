सुताम्रपर्णी जलराशियोगे निबध्य सेतुं विशिखैरसंख्यैः ।
श्रीरामचन्द्रेण समर्पितं तं रामेश्वराख्यं नियतं नमामि ॥

I regularly and daily offer my salutations to Lord Rameshwar, who was established by Lord Shri Ramachandra by building a bridge with countless arrows at the confluence of the Tamraparni River and the ocean.
