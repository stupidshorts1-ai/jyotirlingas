हर हर महादेव!

Shri Kashi Vishwanath

The Light of Eternal Liberation

सानन्दमानन्दवने वसन्तमानन्दकन्दं हतपापवृन्दम् ।
वाराणसीनाथमनाथनाथं श्रीविश्वनाथं शरणं प्रपद्ये ॥

I take refuge in Lord Vishwanath, the Lord of Kashi, who is the embodiment of bliss and resides blissfully in Anandavana, the sacred city of Varanasi, and who destroys all sins; He is the protector of the helpless.
