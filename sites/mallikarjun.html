हर हर महादेव!

Shri Mallikarjun

Where Shiva Abides with Shakti

श्रीशैलशृङ्गे विबुधातिसङ्गे तुलाद्रितुङ्गेऽपि मुदा वसन्तम् ।
तमर्जुनं मल्लिकपूर्वमेकं नमामि संसारसमुद्रसेतुम् ॥

I bow down to the one and only Lord Mallikarjuna, who resides in a joyful state on the peak of Srisailam, which is higher than even the loftiest mountains, where the gods constantly gather, and who is like a bridge to cross the ocean of worldly existence.
