The 12 Jyotirlingas of Mahadev

हर हर महादेव!

Shri Somnath

The Shrine Reborn Through Time

सौराष्ट्रदेशे विशदेऽतिरम्ये ज्योतिर्मयं चन्द्रकलावतंसम् ।
भक्तिप्रदानाय कृपावतीर्णं तं सोमनाथं शरणं प्रपद्ये ॥

I take refuge in Lord Somnath, the embodiment of the Jyotirlinga, who mercifully descended into the extremely beautiful and pure region of Saurashtra to bestow his blessings, and whose forehead is adorned with the moon.
