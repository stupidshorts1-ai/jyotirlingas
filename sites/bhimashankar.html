The 12 Jyotirlingas of Mahadev

हर हर महादेव!

Shri Bhimashankar

The Protector of His Devotees

यं डाकिनिशाकिनिकासमाजे निषेव्यमाणं पिशिताशनैश्च ।
सदैव भीमादिपदप्रसिद्दं तं शङ्करं भक्तहितं नमामि ॥

I bow down to Lord Bhimashankar, the benefactor of his devotees, who is always served by ghosts among the Dakinis and Shakinis.
