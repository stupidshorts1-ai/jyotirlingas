Shri Omkareshwar

The Lord of The Confluence

The photos depict Shri Omkareshwar on the left and Shri Mamleshwar on the right

कावेरिकानर्मदयोः पवित्रे समागमे सज्जनतारणाय ।
सदैवमान्धातृपुरे वसन्तम् ओङ्कारमीशं शिवमेकमीडे ॥

I praise Lord Omkareshwar, the unique bestower of blessings, who resides eternally in the city of Mandhata, near the sacred confluence of the Kaveri and Narmada rivers, to help virtuous souls cross the ocean of worldly existence.
