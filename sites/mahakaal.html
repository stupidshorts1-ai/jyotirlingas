The 12 Jyotirlingas of Mahadev

हर हर महादेव!

Shri Mahakaleshwar

The Lord of Time and Death

अवन्तिकायां विहितावतारं मुक्तिप्रदानाय च सज्जनानाम् ।
अकालमृत्योः परिरक्षणार्थं वन्दे महाकालमहासुरेशम् ॥

I salute Mahadev, popularly known as Mahakaal,
who incarnated in Avantipuri to grant salvation to noble men,
for protection from untimely death.
