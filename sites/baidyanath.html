The 12 Jyotirlingas of Mahadev

हर हर महादेव!

Baba Baidyanath

The Divine Healer

पूर्वोत्तरे प्रज्वलिकानिधाने सदा वसन्तं गिरिजासमेतम् ।
सुरासुराराधितपादपद्मं श्रीवैद्यनाथं तमहं नमामि ॥

I bow to Lord Vaidyanath, who resides eternally in the cremation ground in the northeast direction, within Vaidyanath Dham, always in the company of Mother Parvati, and whose lotus feet are worshipped by both gods and demons.
